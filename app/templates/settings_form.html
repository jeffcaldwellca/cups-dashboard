Upload Certificate & Key

Upload a PEM-encoded certificate and matching private key to enable HTTPS for this dashboard. A server restart is required after uploading for changes to take effect.

Notes
  • Both files must be PEM-encoded. The certificate and key are validated as a matching pair before saving.
  • Intermediate / chain certificates should be appended to the certificate file (bundle).
  • For production deployments a reverse proxy (nginx, Caddy, Traefik) is recommended instead of Flask's built-in SSL.
  • CUPS manages its own certificate separately at /etc/cups/ssl/ — this setting only affects the PaperTrail dashboard.
  • After uploading, restart the dashboard process. The URL will change from http:// to https://.
